Information About me

I am a Data Scientist working in the Aerospace industry, with a keen interest in anything related to Artificial Intelligence. I did my undergraduate studies in Greece and I hold a Master of Engineering (MEng) in Electrical Engineering and Computer science from National Technical University of Athens. My major is in control system and robotics while my other huge interest lies with physics (also a minor in my degree). My studies gave me a solid background in maths, physics and engineering principles, but most importantly they provided me with the mindset of constant search for knowledge and the love of learning. My time at NTUA instilled me with an unquenchable thirst for anything related to technology.

One of the passions that I acquired when majoring in controls and robotics was Artificial Intelligence. I firmly believe it is the revolution of our age and one of the pillars that will thrust technology and society forward. I have a huge interest in all aspects of Artificial Intelligence ranging from classical AI algorithms to Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning. My goal is to constantly put my firm background in math, computer science and programming to the test, seeking and developing novel solutions whilst trying to always learn something new and acquire different viewpoints and approaches to problems that matter.
